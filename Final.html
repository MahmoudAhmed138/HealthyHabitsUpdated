Recommended Solutions

One of the resources that can help you is The Atomic habits book and here some recommendations based on your answers for the assessment :

Break down big goals: A way to avoid setting unrealistic goals is to break down big goals into smaller, manageable steps. This helps you focus on the process, not the outcome, and reduce the risk of feeling overwhelmed or discouraged. For example, if your goal is to run a marathon, you can start by setting a weekly running schedule, increasing your distance and speed gradually, and joining a running club or group.
